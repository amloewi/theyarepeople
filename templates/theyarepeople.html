Dear member of this nation's media,

I recently read some of your work on Donald Trump. Unfortunately, I believe you left out the aspect of this man that most deserves national attention.

"These are not people. Just remember that."

Donald Trump said that, to a vast crowd, at a rally in North Carolina. Talking about people who disagreed with his deep belief -- that there is humanity that can be dismissed. That he can snarl it away, while those around him nod soberly, encouraged.

If there is one thing that makes me human,
it is that I see others as human.

And I will not entertain the threat of a president who does not enshrine this principle. And I will not sit while he is written and talked or even joked about, in a way that allows one to ignore this side of him. So I plead with you now to use your post, and its power, to describe the man as he is.

Thank you
